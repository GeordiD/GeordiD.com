Planning Life

The to-do list that schedules itself.

PlanningLife

Platforms: Android

PlanningLife is a to-do list app designed from the ground up to help you better organize your day. The app features an algorithm to help you schedule your task list and highlight what's really important. The app is set to launch soon on Android and iPhone.

I developed this app as a freelancer for Mountaintop Life. I was the sole developer of the Android app, from simple concept ("to-do list with prioritization algorithm") to final product.
